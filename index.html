Art

Anahy was first introduced to art when she was ten, playing dolls with her cousin. They used paper dolls instead of plastic ones.
"I was entraced with how someone could create an image, a picture with pencil and paper."
That’s how her passion for art ignited. She's practiced multiple drawing styles in order to find her signature one, thus developing her skill even today as a fifteen year old girl.
